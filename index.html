PHYSICIANS

CUSTOMER PROFILE

JOBS PAINS GAINS
JOBS

FUNCTIONALS

- Show respect for their patients: the doctor has to establish a relationship of trust with his patient, for which the sincerity and lack of personal interests are essential.

- Record your work properly: the documents prepared by the doctor must be clear and precise.

- Maintain direct conversations with your patients; this should do everything possible to make clear the message you want to convey to the patient, providing them with the information they need so they can make the best decisions about the care of children.

- Use systems resources correctly to provide the best possible patient care.


SOCIAL

- Be recognized for the good management of their work.

- Win status

PERSONAL

- Feeling at peace with yourself for the good work you do. 

- Sensation of safety in your work: Thanks to you doing a good job you can have the certainty that you will maintain and you could even get a promotion for the work you do.

SUPPORT

- Co-Creator of value: The doctor is able to modify the treatment as he sees fit.

PAINS

Characteristics, problems and unwanted results

• Functionals
Despite the treatment provided, not notice an improvement by the patient.
• Social
Being criticized for not solving the problem.
Being fired for negligence.
• Secondary
Work in a health center that does not have the minimum requirements necessary to work.
Work center far from your home.
• Unwanted sensations
Abandonment of treatment.

Obstacles

• The inconsistency on the part of the patient
• The poor application of treatment by the patient

Risks

• Increase in the prevalence of the disease due to a poor diagnosis or to misdirect treatment.

GAINS



NECESSARY
To correctly identify the type of anemia in order to carry out the treatment.

EXPECTED
Access to the patient's history for a better follow-up.

DESIRED
New methods and products to combat anemia.

UNEXPECTED
Help and personalized monitoring of nurses to facilitate a history that would lead to a better job.

FIT

Problem - solution fit
The doctors are those people who watch over the health for which they are willing and interested in the quality they provide, so, for them anemia is a challenge in which they will do everything possible to manage the disease in a professional manner and safe.

Product - market fit
Since the proposal sounds attractive and benefits both parties, both as a patient and as a doctor, we expect better advice and attention from the doctors when acting.

Business model fit
Thanks to the correct work of the health team it is estimated that the prevalence of anemia would decrease; this would lead to less loss in the GDP of the State and consequently increases in the salary of the health team to motivate them to continue like this and even improve.
